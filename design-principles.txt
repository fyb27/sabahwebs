==============================================================
  SABAHWEBS — DESIGN PRINCIPLES
  A plain-language guide to how this site is designed.
==============================================================

THE ONE BIG IDEA
----------------
Typography carries the weight. Colour is rationed. Depth comes
from shades of grey, not shadows. The page reads top-to-bottom
like a printed manifesto, calm and confident — a "designer's
midnight gallery."


THE CORE PRINCIPLES
-------------------
1. RESTRAINT OVER DECORATION
   One accent colour. One filled button per screen. No gradients
   or effects used as filler. If something isn't earning its place,
   it's removed.

2. HIERARCHY THROUGH TYPE, NOT BOXES
   Size, weight and spacing create order before any container does.
   Big quiet headlines, small technical labels.

3. WHISPER, DON'T SHOUT
   Headlines are light weight (400), not bold. Confidence at full
   volume rather than shouting. Bold is reserved only for tiny
   uppercase labels (OUR CLIENTS, WHY CHOOSE US).

4. FLAT, ARCHITECTURAL SURFACES
   No drop shadows. Depth is built from a 4-step grey stack and
   thin 1px hairline borders — like signage on a dark wall, not
   cards floating in space.

5. WHITESPACE IS A FEATURE
   Generous, consistent spacing between sections. Content is given
   room to breathe so the eye always knows where to rest.

6. EARN THE COLOUR
   The single warm accent (a soft amber) appears at most once per
   screen — the word "Sabah", the recommended price, the active
   client. Everything else is greyscale, so the accent always means
   something.

7. MOTION WITH PURPOSE
   Animation is fast, smooth and consistent — a gentle fade-and-rise
   as things scroll into view, subtle lifts and slides on hover.
   Never bouncy, never gratuitous. Anyone who prefers less motion
   gets a still, calm version automatically.

8. FAST BY DESIGN
   Hand-built and lightweight, with modern compressed images, so it
   loads quickly even on mobile data. Speed is part of the design,
   not an afterthought.


THE PALETTE
-----------
   Background ........ warm near-black  (#101010)
   Deepest canvas .... #080808
   Card surface ...... #212121 / #333333
   Main text ......... soft off-white   (#f3f3f3)
   Muted text ........ greys            (#949494 / #888888)
   THE accent ........ amber whisper     (#e7c59a)  — used sparingly

   Note: the black is warm (#101010), never a harsh pure black.


THE TYPE
--------
   Headlines & UI .... Inter, light weight (400)
   Labels & numbers .. JetBrains Mono — only for small technical
                       labels, categories, timestamps
   Letter-spacing .... always slightly tight (negative), never loose
   Sizing ............ a fixed scale (13px up to 63px); fluid sizing
                       so headlines scale smoothly on phones


SHAPE & SPACE
-------------
   Corners ........... just three, used with meaning:
                       8px  = buttons & badges
                       20px = cards
                       99px = pill "chat" button
   Container ......... wide (1560px), centred, with comfortable edges
   Rhythm ............ large, even gaps between sections
   Long reading ...... blog articles kept narrow (~720px) for comfort


SIGNATURE MOMENTS
-----------------
   THE HERO
   A dot-matrix Mount Kinabalu drawn live on screen, drifting at a
   different speed as you scroll (parallax) so it feels like depth,
   not a flat picture. The headline sits above; the mountain rises
   below.

   THE CLIENT LIST
   Not a grid of screenshots — an editorial index of client names.
   Hover one and the rest dim, the name turns amber, and a live
   screenshot of their site floats in following your cursor. On a
   phone it becomes a clean stacked list with the screenshots inline.


PRINCIPLES THAT PROTECT THE SITE
--------------------------------
   ACCESSIBLE      Proper headings, labels on icons, readable
                   contrast, and a reduced-motion mode.
   RESPONSIVE      Adapts cleanly from wide desktop to phone.
   SEO-SAFE        Existing blog links/URLs preserved exactly so no
                   search ranking is lost.
   CONSISTENT      One set of design "tokens" controls colour, type
                   and spacing everywhere — change once, change all.
