FAQ

Web design in KK: common questions.

How much do your websites cost?

A single page is RM500 and a small-business site of up to three pages is RM1,000, one-time and flat. Bigger projects such as more pages, e-commerce or a CMS are scoped together so you know the price up front.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most small sites are ready in a week or two once we have your text and photos. The timeline mainly depends on how quickly we get your content, not the build itself.

Do you do SEO as well as web design?

Yes. Every site ships search-ready, and we also offer ongoing local SEO across Sabah, from Google Business Profile setup to on-page optimisation.

Do you work with businesses outside Kota Kinabalu?

Yes. We work with businesses across Sabah. Everything can be done over WhatsApp, email and video call, so where you are isn't a barrier.

Can you update the site after it launches?

Of course. We're a small local team you can reach directly, so changes and small additions are easy to sort out whenever you need them.

I'm not sure how many pages I need. Can you advise?

Tell us about your business and we'll recommend the smallest site that does the job, even if that's a single page. More on why a small business doesn't need a big website.
