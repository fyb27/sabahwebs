24-Hour Clinic Near Jalan Penampang, Kota Kinabalu — Permai Polyclinics Fortuna

17 May 20265 min read

If you live or work around Jalan Penampang, Taman Fortuna, or Lorong Seroja in Kota Kinabalu, having a reliable clinic that is open around the clock can make a real difference. Permai Polyclinics Fortuna, located at the Fortuna Commercial Centre along Jalan Penampang, is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with no appointment needed. Whether it is a fever that spikes at 3am, a workplace injury, or a routine health screening you keep putting off, you can simply walk in and be seen by a doctor.

About Permai Polyclinics

Permai Polyclinics is one of Sabah's most established private healthcare groups. It was founded in 1997 by Datuk Dr. James Jeremiah in Kota Kinabalu, with a mission to bring quality, accessible healthcare to communities across the state. In 2012 the group earned the ISO 9001 Quality Management certification, a mark of its consistent standards in patient care. The Fortuna branch is one of the newer additions to the network, bringing that same trusted, round-the-clock care to the fast-growing Jalan Penampang and Penampang corridor.

Where Exactly Is It?

The clinic is situated at Ground Floor, Lot 3, Block D2, Lorong Seroja 2, Fortuna Commercial Centre, Mile 2.5, Jalan Penampang, 88200 Kota Kinabalu, Sabah. It is easy to find, sitting right inside the Fortuna Commercial Centre and accessible straight off the main Jalan Penampang road, roughly 2.5 miles from the city centre. Free parking is available within the commercial centre, so dropping by even late at night is hassle free. What Services Do They Offer?

Permai Polyclinics Fortuna offers a comprehensive range of general healthcare services, making it a one-stop clinic for individuals and families in the area:

  • General consultation
  • Blood pressure and blood glucose checks
  • Urine and blood tests
  • Vaccination for all ages
  • Health screening and medical check-ups
  • Pre-employment medical examinations
  • Wound dressing and treatment
  • IV drip and injections
  • Nebuliser treatment
  • Ultrasound services
  • Knee injections for osteoarthritis
  • Sick leave and medical certificates
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, cholesterol)
  • Paediatric consultation

Whether you need a routine check-up, treatment for a sudden illness, or a medical certificate for work, the clinic handles it all under one roof. On-site lab testing and an in-house pharmacy mean that in most cases you can be diagnosed, treated, and walk out with your medication without needing to travel anywhere else.

Health Screening and Medical Check-Ups

Beyond walk-in treatment, the clinic is well set up for preventive care. Health screening packages cover the numbers every adult should keep an eye on: blood pressure, blood glucose, cholesterol, kidney and liver function, and a full blood count. Pre-employment and pre-marital medical examinations are also available, both of which are commonly required by Sabah employers and government departments. Catching conditions like high blood pressure, diabetes, or high cholesterol early is far easier, cheaper, and safer than treating the complications later on.

Open 24 Hours, Walk-Ins Welcome

One of the biggest advantages of Permai Polyclinics Fortuna is its 24-hour operation. Unlike most clinics in Kota Kinabalu that close by early evening, this clinic keeps a doctor on duty at any hour, whether it is the middle of the night, the early hours of the morning, a weekend, or a public holiday. No appointment is required. That matters most in exactly the moments you cannot plan for, such as a child running a high fever overnight, a deep cut that needs stitching or dressing, a sudden allergic reaction, or an asthma flare. Knowing there is a clinic nearby that never closes gives local families real peace of mind.

When Should You Visit a 24-Hour Clinic?

A 24-hour walk-in clinic is ideal for urgent but non-emergency problems that still cannot wait until morning. Common reasons people come in include high fever, food poisoning, vomiting and diarrhoea, minor wounds and burns, sprains and minor injuries, ear, throat and eye infections, urinary discomfort, and getting a medical certificate. For genuine life-threatening emergencies such as chest pain, stroke symptoms, difficulty breathing, or major trauma, you should still go straight to a hospital emergency department or call 999. For almost everything in between, a walk-in clinic is faster, more affordable, and far less crowded than a hospital emergency department.

Highly Rated by Patients

Since opening, Permai Polyclinics Fortuna has quickly built a strong reputation among patients in the Jalan Penampang area, backed by a steady stream of positive Google reviews and a near-perfect star rating. Patients regularly highlight the clean and modern environment, friendly and professional staff, short waiting times, and the simple convenience of being able to see a doctor at any hour of the day or night.
