Who Is ProSteel Sdn Bhd?

ProSteel is a Sabah-based construction materials company specialising in scaffolding solutions across KK and the wider region. They offer sales, rental, and buyback of scaffolding and accessories. So whether you're a contractor yang perlukan equipment for one project or a company building a long-term inventory, ProSteel has an arrangement that works for you.

Their warehouse is located at Pusat Perindustrian Sepanggar, putting them right in the heart of KK's industrial zone for fast delivery to your site.

Scaffolding(Sales & Rental)ProSteel's core product is their tubular scaffolding system, strong and versatile, suitable for everything from low-rise shophouse renovations to large commercial builds. They supply complete systems including all accessories like couplers, base plates, U-heads, cross braces, and ledgers. Semua ada.

Scaffolding AccessoriesNeed to top up your existing system? ProSteel stocks a full range of fittings so you're never short mid-project.

Modular CabinsSite office, guardhouse, or mobile toilet, ProSteel supplies all three. Practical and ready to use, these units help keep your site compliant and operational from hari pertama.

Everlast Aluminium LaddersFrom standard double-sided ladders to heavy-duty and fibreglass extension models for electrical work, their ladder range covers every access need on site.

Safety Netting & FormworkFor perimeter protection and concrete forming work, ProSteel also carries safety netting and formwork materials.

Scaffolding Rental in KK, How It Works

Renting from ProSteel is straightforward. You bagitau them your project scope, they advise on the right quantity and type, deliver to your site, and collect everything when the job is done. No long-term commitment, no need to pening kepala pasal storing equipment you don't need.

This makes rental especially popular with sub-contractors in KK who don't want to tie up modal in equipment between projects.

Buy or Rent? Their Buyback Option Makes It Easy

One of ProSteel's standout offerings is their buyback programme. You purchase the scaffolding outright, use it across your projects, and when you're done, ProSteel buys it back. It's a lower-risk way to own your own equipment without worrying about idle stock sitting in your yard.

For larger contractors managing multiple sites across Sabah, this kind of flexibility memang sangat membantu.
